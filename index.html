About the author, Mike Moore

Paine: How We Dismantled the FBI In Our Pajamas is a journey to the truth, a scandal uncovered at the center of power—and a personal comeback from hell.

A decade ago, a SWAT-clad team of FBI agents with automatic weapons stormed into the Philadelphia home of award-winning journalist Mike Moore. They terrorized his wife and young children at gunpoint after rousting them out of bed. And then they did it again, a second time.

Next, FBI top brass went on an obsessive rampage to ruin Moore’s professional career. They even prevented him from attending his mother’s funeral.

What did Mike Moore know that so terrified the FBI they would stoop so low?

In response, Mike Moore took on the pen-name of ‘Thomas Paine’, assembled his journalism crew of old-school reporters, and in mid-2016 launched the ground-breaking news website, True Pundit.

Week by week, scoop by scoop, True Pundit has lifted the lid on a series of scandals that have seen the top tiers of the FBI crumble in full, public sight.

Once-untouchable FBI leaders have been fired. Others quit. And the FBI itself is now the target of numerous federal investigations.

All this Mike ‘Thomas Paine’ Moore and his crew did with pens and laptops while working from home, often in pajamas.

As the True Pundit team dug deeper, knocked on more doors, reached out to ever more well-placed contacts and intel veterans, the threads of a new story began to come together.

It was a story so shocking that an entire, 400-page book was needed to reveal the truth, document the crimes in detail, and expose in full the culprits for their crimes against America.

The book’s title? Paine: How We Dismantled the FBI In Our Pajamas.
